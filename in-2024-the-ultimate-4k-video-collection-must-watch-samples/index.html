Recommended size: The most recommended size to upload videos on YouTube is 1920 by 1080p but it also allows uploading videos in 2560 by 1440p and 3840 by 2160p.

Minimum width: YouTube allows a minimum width of 426 by 240p.

Supported aspect ratios: On YouTube the supported aspect ratios are 16:9 and 4:3.

Part 1: What is the Bokeh Effect and Why Should I Be Using it?

The word Bokehor boke refers to the Japanese concept of blur. It is, in its simplest form, a style of photography where one object is the primary focus, and everything else is strategically blurred.

Using the Bokeh effect is an excellent way to separate the focal point of your photo from non-focal points. It not only serves the purpose of highlighting the focus of the image, but can also help when youre looking to get rid of an unpleasant or unwanted background.
