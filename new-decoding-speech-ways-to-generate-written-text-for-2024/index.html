Top 10 Movie Trailer Apps for iPhones and iPads

Price: Free

iMovie offers a variety of predesigned trailer themes, so you just have to pick the one you like the most and then choose the footage you want to include in your trailer. Videos that are saved on the devices camera roll can be easily imported into the app, which means that you can start making a new trailer just moments after recording the footage.
